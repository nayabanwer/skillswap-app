How Learning Exchanges Work

Exchange Process:
  1. Send Request
    Message someone and propose a skill exchange
  2. Plan Together
    Discuss schedule, format, and expectations
  3. Exchange Skills
    Meet online or in-person to share knowledge
  4. Leave Reviews
    Rate your experience and help others
Learning Formats:
  • Video Calls: One-on-one or group sessions via your preferred video platform
  • In-Person: Meet locally for hands-on learning experiences
  • Text Chat: Quick tips, Q&A, and ongoing mentorship
  • Resource Sharing: Exchange guides, tutorials, and materials

Safety & Best Practices

Do's:
  • Be clear about your learning goals and teaching approach
  • Start with public places for in-person meetings
  • Set boundaries and expectations upfront
  • Be patient and respectful with learning paces
  • Leave honest reviews after exchanges
Don'ts:
  • Don't share personal financial information
  • Don't meet in private locations initially
  • Don't pressure for immediate commitments
  • Don't ignore red flags or uncomfortable situations
  • Don't exchange money for skills (keep it skill-for-skill)
